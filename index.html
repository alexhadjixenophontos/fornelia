Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long does it take to cook meals with Fornelia Mini Open?

    We have designed the Fornelia Mini Open having in mind that a conventional oven takes 3 hours to well cook 4.2 KG of meat. So, when loaded at 4.2 Kg of meat the time to cook is 3 hours. If we put only 2 Kg the time is around 2 hours during a sunny day. It will take little more (30 minutes) if the sun comes and goes at 50% of the time. For fish it will take one hour and for bread aslo 1 hour.

  • Is Fornelia Mini Open easy to transfer?

    The Fornelia Mini Open when closed looks like a small suitcase and is ease to take anywhere, to the beach to the mountain to your balcony or your boat.

  • How easy is it to use the Fornelia Mini Open?

    Very easy to use, you just have to put the oven on the base, direct it towards the sun and fill the round tray with the food you want to cook, insert it in the oven and take care every 20 minutes to redirect it towards the sun. You dont have to worry for burning the food.

  • What can you cook with the Fornelia Mini Open?

    You can cook all kinds of food from bread, pasta, vegetables to meat and fish but also cakes. Soon we will have a recipe book with more instructions.

  • How do you clean the Fornelia Mini Open?

    You mast take care not to hit the oven and especially the vacuum tube. To clean it always use any glass surface non bleach liquid spray and soft paper tissue. The vacuum tube mast not be too hot while cleaning.

  • If something breaks on the Fornelia Mini Open how can I fix it?

    The Fornelia Mini Open has a modular design and any damaged part can be replaced. Ask our local representative or directly our head office for any support.
